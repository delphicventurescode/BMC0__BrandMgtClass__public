# Tech Tip: Use Perplexity AI for Smarter Research

**What it is:**  
[Perplexity AI](https://www.perplexity.ai) is a powerful research assistant that provides concise, well-sourced answers to your questions. It’s ideal for students working on brand case studies or market analyses.

**Why it’s useful for students:**  
- Gives you summarized, evidence-based responses with sources attached.  
- Helps you discover new academic papers, articles, and real-world examples.  
- Cuts down time spent searching across multiple tabs.  
- You can verify every claim directly from cited sources.

**Pro Tip:**  
When researching for your Brand Management project, try asking:  
“Compare Coca-Cola’s and Pepsi’s brand positioning using recent data.”  
Then review the citations to ensure your report includes credible references.
